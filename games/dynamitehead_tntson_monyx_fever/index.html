YO! HIT THE SURF! DYNAMITEHEAD TNTSON is a cartoony Beat 'em up side-scroller, starring the hottest bounty hunter superstar out there - DYNAMITEHEAD.


He's got fire on his head, and BIG money on his mind. PUNCH, SMASH, CRASH your way through deranged otherworldly locations. Clear out entire screens worth of badheads. Take down GetTheBadGuy.net's most NOTORIOUS troublemakers. And GET PAID! Beating up undead freaks has NEVER been this fun before!


THIS JUST IN! Lanky Larry, head of the "Smile :)" cartel, and his pesky henchmen decided to hijack the entirety of Groovy Graveyard. EVERYONE'S INVITED! DIAL-M (DynamiteHead's sentient phone buddy, always on the lookout) forwards this info towards our eponymous bounty hunter, and before you know it - They're both in for the deal.


Getting through Groovy Graveyard ain't gonna be as easy as walking around with a basket on your head. Fumble this one, and you'll have one beautiful smile carved onto your face. So what do you do? YOU SHOW 'EM WHAT'S UP! Give those badheads a kick or two, fill up your TNT JUICE meter, and turn the whole place UPSIDE DOWN! If you feel like you need help with the moves, give Instruction Manual a good read.
