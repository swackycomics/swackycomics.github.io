DYNAMITEHEAD TNTSON - The Toxic Demo

Date released: September 8, 2024

Platforms: Windows, HTML5

Status: In development

YO! HIT THE SURF! DYNAMITEHEAD TNTSON is an energetic Beat 'em up side-scroller with wacky cartoony animation, zany humor, and gritty undertones.

YOU play as DynamiteHead, Crypt Plains' hottest superstar. He's got fire on his head, and BIG money on his mind.

One day, DynamiteHead receives a message from DIAL-M, saying there's trouble in the Toxic Trenches. Some chit-chatting later, and it turns out FRANKENBARF's out for absolute DESTRUCTION! Finding out how much the mission pays, DynamiteHead goes headfirst into explosive action. THAT'LL SHOW 'EM!
