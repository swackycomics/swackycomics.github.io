JLFJ (May 2020 Beta)

Date released: May, 2020

Platforms: Windows

Status: Cancelled

Reason for cancellation

Due to the way this game stores levels, the more I added, the more you had to wait until you could get into the stage. I then decided to remake the game a few months later (See: Late 2020 Beta).

Lo and behold, the FIRST EVER version of Jimmy's Lost Fruits Journey ever made!

Those pesky fruit stealers stole Jim's fruit stash! And now it's all up to Jimmy to get things sorted out. Adventure through Fruit Plains, the Crazy Desert, and just a tiny bit of the third snow world.
