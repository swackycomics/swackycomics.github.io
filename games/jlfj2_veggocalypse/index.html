Date released: January 6, 2023

Platforms: Windows

Game engine: GameMaker: Studio 2

Reason for cancellation

The main reason is, to me, this game seemed boring and generic. Overscoping. Halfway through development I've lost any motivation to work on this game.

Jimmy's Lost Fruits Journey 2: Veggocalypse is an Earthworm Jim and Jazz Jackrabbit 2 inspired shoot-em-up platformer with fluent & wacky animation, high speed moveset, and lots of fruit filled action!

Uh-oh!! Dr. Vegg's up to no good. And this time, he's NOT fooling around! Calling in Mr. Plot for help, they have decided to turn Jim's life into a living NIGHTMARE. With help of Mr. Plot's son, DynamiteHead, AppleBot has malfunctioned and started causing absolute chaos and destruction in the Fruit Plains. Jim, after finding out what's going on, went after AppleBot to stop all this havoc. Will he succeed in his journey? There's only one way to find out.
