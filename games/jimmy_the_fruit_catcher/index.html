Jimmy The Fruit Catcher

Date released: January 15, 2023

Platforms: Sega Mega Drive (Sega Genesis)

Dev kit: SGDK 1.70

Status: Complete

Welcome to Jimmy The Fruit Catcher! Play as the eponymous protagonist Jimmy and catch fruits flying straight from the sky!

Your objective is to get the highest possible score by getting fruits, hearts and time! When you lose, your hi-score will be displayed on the screen. Think you can max it out?

Beware of the pesky projectiles though! Those things can get you a quick Game Over if you don't know what you're doing. It's best to avoid them.
