RedBidd: A Colorful Revival

Date released: February 18, 2022

Last update: May 17, 2022

Platforms: Windows

Status: Cancelled

Reason for cancellation

This game was just fundamentally flawed and not fun to play. This, coupled with some awful code in the game, and the fact that I was still using GameMaker: Studio 1, made me ultimately cancel this game.

RedBidd: A Colorful Revival is a complete remake of the original "RedBidd: A Squared Madness" made by swackyGCF in GameMaker: Studio. As of now, the game features TWO whole worlds full of levels with its own gimmicks and tricks.

You play as RedBidd, a red rabbit who likes spending time with his best (and only) friend Wraps playing video games. However, one day, a glowing beam broke out of nowhere and made the entire world lose anything resembling color. Now it's time for RedBidd to find out what's going on. In this game you'll find yourself fighting a giant mouse, exploring dangerous caves, alleyways and even a pitch-black void! This adventure is one of its kind.
