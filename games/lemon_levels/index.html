Lemon Levels

Date released: August 24, 2023

Platforms: Windows

Game engine: GameMaker

Status: Cancelled

Reason for cancellation

Loss of motivation. Latter half of 2023 was a rough period in terms of my game development endeavors.

When life gives you lemons, there's only ONE thing you've oughta do - Make your own level.

Welcome to LEMON LEVELS. Take a crack at making your own level OR play other people's levels! Confused? Good. This game takes brains to figure out, you know. You sure you're up for it?

I had bigger plans for this one - An actual level sharing tab, more gameplay stuff and whatnot.

Check out the included tutorial.json file to get started. If you feel like messing around with the editor, click the second button in the menu. Be aware that .json saving and loading might not work for unknown reasons.
