Terms (Commissions)

Contact:

You can contact me through discord (swackygcf), or by emailing me at swackygcfkofi@gmail.com. I also accept requests through Newgrounds DMs (swackygcf.newgrounds.com).


Payment:

Contact me first with commission details before paying upfront.

Once the artwork has been sketched, you will be asked to pay the full price. USD is the preferred currency.


What I offer:

Depending on the commission, you will get:

- A fully finished/rendered drawing. Since I'm using a vector-based program (Flash 8), it can be scaled to any resolution of your liking.

If you're requesting a rendered drawing, specify the resolution beforehand, as most often than not I make those in a raster-based program (e.g. Photoshop).

- A frame-by-frame animation (Typically animated in the range of 12-18 fps).

- A multi-page comic.

Refer to commission listing for more details on the price.


Turnaround time:

Static illustrations will take about a day to complete.

Depending on the length, a comic/animation will be completed in the range of a couple days to a couple weeks.

I'll let you know if I need more time to complete the final artwork.


Revisions:

During the sketching phase, you can request as many revisions as you want for free. Once the artwork is nearing completion, extra changes are $2 each.

Significant changes to the artwork will cost more.


Refunds:

Refunds are not offered once the artwork is delivered.


Usage rights:

You will be able to use the artwork for both personal and commercial uses. Free of any additional charges or royalties.


Additional terms:

I'm not taking NSFW requests.
